Popular Destination in Mahakumbh

Triveni Sangam

Triveni Sangam is a Sacred site in prayagraj,india where three rivers meet: Ganga,yamuna,Saraswati.

Lakshmi Ghat

Lakshmi Ghat is a significant cultural and spiritual landmark in paryagraj, attracting visitors from all over india and abrod.

Kumbh Mela Grounds

The Kumbh Mela grounds are a testament to india's rich cultural heritage and its ability to host massive gatherings with ease.

Bade Hanuman Ji Temple

Bade Hanuman ji temple is a must-visit destination for those seeking spiritual enligtenment and a glimpse into india's rich cultural heritage.

Alopi Devi Temple

Alopi Devi Temple is a revered hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Alopi,also known as Goddess Leelavati, situated in prayagraj,india.

Someshwar Mahadev Temple

Someshwar Mahadev Temple is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, situated in prayagraj,india

Anand Bhawan

The former residence of jawaharlal Nehru,the first prime Minister of india,now converted into a museum.

Allahabad Fort

A 16th-century fort built by the Mughal Emperor Akbar,featuring stunning architecture and historical significant.
